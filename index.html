CAF

TCDD has 12 CAF high-speed train sets in its fleet. The first high-speed train sets in Turkey belong to the Spanish CAF company. Each CAF set has 6 wagons.

Siemens

The number of Velaro sets produced by the German manufacturer Siemens reached 19 with the 12-unit order delivered in February 2021. Siemens sets have 8 wagons.

TCDD

Turkey State Railways (TCDD) is the main railway infrastructure operator in Turkey. The Turkish State Railways is responsible for the planning, construction and maintenance of railway infrastructure. The TCDD was formed on 4 June 1929 as the State Railways Administration.
